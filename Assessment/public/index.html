My home town

Croydon - founded 1276

"We put the fun in 'Help, we need funding'"

Croydon is one of the largest commercial districts in Greater London, with a huge shopping district.

The entire town had a population of 192,064 as of 2011, whilst the wider borough had a population of 384,837.

- source wiki


Croydon is quite a large area, but thanks to it's frequent public transport, namely buses and trams, it doesn't take long to get anywhere. Alongside those, Croydon also has a train system that makes it easy to get to anywhere in greater London, taking less than an hour to get to central London from Croydon train station.
