Places to visit

Heart of gaming is an arcade in east croydon. You pay for entry at the door and then have all day to play the games inside at no aditional cost. They've everything from classic arcade games to newer games on playstation, first person shooters and ddr.
Box parl is a food and retail park in east croydon. It's filled with pubs, resturants, fast food places and a few shops.
Croywall is a croydon branch of indoor climbing centre for london (lcc). It's a bouldering gym, boldering is rock climbing without a harness and yes it's completely safe indoors. Students get discounted entry and free shoe hire on specific days of the week, varies with the centre


There are lots of things to do in Croydon: parks to walk in, shops to browse, get stabbed or try to avoid it. Never a dull day with high knife crime.
